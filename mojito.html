Mojito

Necessary ingredients
  • White rum 50ml
  • Sugar syrup 50ml
  • Soda 100ml
  • Lime 80g
  • Mint 3g
  • Crushed ice 200g
Cocktail recipe
  • 1. Put 3 slices of lime in a highball and crush with a mudler
  • 2. Take 10 mint leaves in one hand and slap them with the other hand
  • 3. Put mint in a highball
  • 4. Fill a glass with crushed ice to the top
  • 5. Add sugar syrup 15 ml and white rum 50 ml
  • 6. Top up the soda and gently stir with a cocktail spoon
  • 7. Add a little crushed ice
  • 8. Garnish with a sprig of mint and a slice of lime
