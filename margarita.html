Margarita

Necessary ingredients
  • Silver Tequila 50ml
  • Triple sec Fruko Schulz 25ml
  • Sugar syrup 10ml
  • Lime juice 30ml
  • Lime 10g
  • Salt 2g
  • Ice cubes 200g
Cocktail recipe
  • 1. Make a salty rim on the glass
  • 2. Pour lime juice, sugar syrup, triple sec liqueur and silver tequila into a shaker
  • 3. Fill the shaker with ice cubes and beat
  • 4. Pour through a strainer into a chilled margarita glass
  • 5. Decorate with a lime circle
