Cosmopolitan

Necessary ingredients
  • Citrus vodka Tsarskaya 40ml
  • Triple sec Fruko Schulz 20ml
  • Cranberry juice 50ml
  • Lime juice 10ml
  • Orange peel 1 piece
  • Ice cubes 200g
Cocktail recipe
  • 1. Pour lime juice, cranberry juice, triple sec liqueur and citrus vodka into a shaker
  • 2. Fill the shaker with ice cubes and beat
  • 3. Pour through a strainer into a chilled cocktail glass
  • 4. Light a burner over the cocktail and squeeze the oil from the orange peel on it
