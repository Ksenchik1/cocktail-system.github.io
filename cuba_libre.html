Cuba libre

Necessary ingredients
  • Golden rum 50ml
  • Lime juice 10ml
  • Cola 140ml
  • Lime 20g
  • Ice cubes 180g
Cocktail recipe
  • 1. Fill the highball with ice cubes to the top
  • 2. Pour lime juice and golden rum
  • 3. Top up the cola and gently stir with a cocktail spoon
  • 4. Decorate with lime mugs
