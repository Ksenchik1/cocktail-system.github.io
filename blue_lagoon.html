Blue lagoon

Necessary ingredients
  • Vodka Royal 50ml
  • Blue Curacao liqueur Fruko Schulz 20ml
  • Sprite 150ml
  • Pineapple 30g
  • Ice cubes 200g
Cocktail recipe
  • 1. Fill the hurricane with ice cubes to the top
  • 2. Pour blue curacao liqueur and vodka
  • 3. Top up the sprite and gently stir with a cocktail spoon
  • 4. Decorate with a slice of pineapple
